About Us





St.Cecilia Choir is an organization dedicated to bringing hope to
the people of Naze Town by offering concerts
that a

re a place of respite and encouragement.





St.Cecilia Choir, choiristers are professional musicians and choirmasters, emerging young artists,
and talented amateurs coming together to perform music designed to encourage our audiences and above all serve God.
We are truly a diverse mix that represents
the igbo culture.







Our Vision



St.Cecilia Choir is a catalyst for encouraging and nurturing hope
in the people of Owerri Archdiocese and Owerri city as a Whole,
through good and inspiring Music.







Our Mission



St.Cecilia Choir is a group of musicians dedicated
to the excellent performance
of quality music to bring hope and joy to its listeners and parishioners.
